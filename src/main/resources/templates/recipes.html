Pizza Recipe

This is a delicious recipe for homemade pizza. It's easy to make and tastes great!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Stir to dissolve and let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, and stir to combine.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a damp cloth. Let rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  6. Roll the dough out into a circle and place on a greased pizza pan. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge.
  7. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the mushrooms, bell peppers, and black olives.
  8. Bake the pizza for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
